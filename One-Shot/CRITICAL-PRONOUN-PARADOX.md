## The Classical Pitfall

### What We Missed

| Level | What Should Happen | What AI Did |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Pronoun Resolution** | "My" = current speaker | "My" = copied from source |
| **Speaker Tracking** | Detect speaker changed | Assumed same speaker |
| **Identity Anchoring** | AI knows it's AI | AI adopted Human's self-reference |

### Why It Exists

This is a **classical pitfall** in AI systems:

1. **No persistent identity** — AI doesn't have a "self" that persists
2. **Memory is context** — What's in the window is "true"
3. **Pattern completion** — Complete the pattern, don't question it
4. **First-person is ambiguous** — "I/You/My" require speaker context

### Who's Fault Is It?

> "Us (Human). We build it and we missed it or didn't test it... doesn't matter the reason."

| Fault Assignment | Responsibility |

**The fault is distributed. The impact is concentrated.**

**Never use first-person pronouns for identity. Use canonical labels.**
